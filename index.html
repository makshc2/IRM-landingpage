  • Participation in a clinical trial requires many efforts from patients and our goal is to ensure study participants are assisted and their burden is alleviated regarding both the making of travel arrangements to and from their study visits and the subsequent time and costs incurred.
  • Implementation of Patient Travel and Transportation Service to the study is a positive leverage to patient enrollment, compliance and drop-out rate.
  • Arrangement of patients travel to the site and back with the focus on safety, promptness and reliability will expedite patients retention in your clinical trials, increase compliance with trial schedule and reduce workload of study coordinators.

Company Overview

IRM is a service company dedicated to provision of tailor-made solutions to support clinical trials and to make them more efficient. Our expertise in working in this specific market with both industry and health care providers results in superior service, cost savings, and more efficient clinical trial conduct.

Company Mission Statement

IRM mission is to make clinical research done better,
faster, more efficient and with robust results
IRM is the power that moves clinical research further.

Company Philosophy and Vision

To achieve sustainable growth we, at IRM, established the following vision statements:
To improve clinical research by enhancing the industry with tailored solutions
To become a trusted partner for the industry
To become a great place to work where people are inspired to be the best they can be.

About IRM

We are pleased to support all parties involved into the clinical trial process: patients, investigators, monitors.
IRM is dedicated on provision of tailored solutions to enhance execution of Clinical Trials. We are focused on solutions for Patients and Investigators to:
• accelerate enrollment
• generate interest in the study
• increase retention
• help Pharma and CRO staff to execute trials better
